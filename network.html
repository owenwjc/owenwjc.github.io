Supervised Trading on Key Sentiment (STonKS)

An Exploratory Analysis of 2.39 million Reddit Comments

Over the past year over 10 million comments have been made on reddit.com/r/wallstreetbets discussion posts.
I took 30% of them and ended up with just under 2.4 million comments that could be marked with a sentiment value. Of which over 430 thousand comments contained at least one stock ticker or company mention and 61 thousand contained at least two tickers or mentions. Within the 61 thousand comments containing two or more stock tickers 2700 unique companies were mentioned. I chose to keep companies that had at least 25 mentions which took the network down to 350 nodes and 30 thousand edges.
